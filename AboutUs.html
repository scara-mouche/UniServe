About Us

Uniserve is an exclusive online platform created to provide EARIST students with easy access to high-quality, school-approved uniforms. Designed with convenience in mind, Uniserve streamlines the ordering process to ensure students can prepare for the academic year without the hassle of traditional uniform shopping.

We offer a wide range of officially accepted uniform pieces tailored specifically for EARIST requirements. Students can place their orders online and simply pick them up at the EARIST campus — saving time and avoiding long lines.

  • ✅ For EARIST students only
  • ✅ Official and school-compliant uniform designs
  • ✅ Easy online ordering
  • ✅ Campus pick-up only (No delivery)

At Uniserve, we are committed to making the uniform purchasing process simpler, faster, and more efficient for the EARIST community. Your journey starts here — ready to wear, ready to learn.
